What Pulse captures
Everything that happens
between kickoff and closure.
Stakeholders
Every person involved — name, role, side. Sponsor changes, new contacts, silent stakeholders. All documented.
Project journal
A chronological log of everything — meetings, decisions, risks, escalations, raw notes. Paste from email or Slack.
Milestones
Key dates per project — sign-offs, training sessions, go/no-go reviews. Visible on the roadmap, tracked to completion.
Risk flags
Log risks the moment you spot them. Track resolution. Know what's open, what's escalated, what's been mitigated.
Health score
A live score across alignment, confidence, adoption and timeline. Updated as you progress. Tells you where the project is, not where it should be.
AI debrief
One click. Pulse reads the full project history and generates a structured brief — situation, risks, next actions, opening line for your next call.
